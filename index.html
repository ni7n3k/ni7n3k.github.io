What We Do

yellowpolygon specializes in building custom Virtual Reality (VR) experiences for the real estate industry.

yellowpolygon creates VR worlds that give your clients a fully immersive experience like no other. Visualizing a property that hasn't even been built yet, VR can connect your audience with your product both emotionally and physically, enhancing your marketing capabilities and giving consumers greater confidence to purchase off-the-plan.

How We Do It

Virtual Reality is revolutionizing architecture and yellowpolygon takes VR to devices like the Oculus Rift of the Samsung Gear VR. It creates an experience that immerses you in the scene instead of looking at it. The experience really has to be seen to be believed, everything looks real and life size. Our clients are blown away the first time they put one of these on.

The Process

The process is simple:

  • - You provide us with 2D drawings or 3D models of your project.
  • - We build the VR application from the documents you provide.
  • - We equip you with the technology to immerse your clients in the scenes.
  • - You wow your clients !
The Technology

We create the most immersive experiences using:

  • - Immersive Mobile & Tethered VR Technology.
  • - 3D Rendered Models and CGI.
  • - 2D/ 3D / 360° Photos & Videos.
  • - 3D Rendered Models and CGI.
